Artist Credits:
Uploads use Artist credits. Two free credits are provided for the tutorial (first upload).

Additional Artist credits are available as an in-app purchase in small, medium or large packs: small pack of Artist credits (10 credits) costs $0.99
medium pack of Artist credits (100 credits) costs $3.99…under 5 cents per credit
large pack of Artist credits (3000 credits)…under 1 cent per credit

An Artist credit is a consumable product. Consumable products, by their nature, aren’t synced or restored. Please understand that, for example, buying ten credits on an iPhone doesn’t also give ten credits on an iPad, and vice versa.

Artist Gallery:
We curate Brand Galleries to select outstanding images to be featured in the Artist Gallery on the Mane Page social network for a limited time of 60 days.

My Artwork & Brand Galleries:
Brand Galleries on Mane Page are collections of artwork that portray the artist’s interpretation of a brand’s philosophy, lifestyle and image. Brand Galleries are viewable on a secure web site that only brand owners can access. Artists can go to Upload Artwork >> My Artwork to view their past uploads. Brand owners can view all artwork in their galleries. Copyright for uploaded artwork is controlled by the artist and artists keep 100% of the proceeds from artwork rights & permissions sold to a brand owner. We do not take a cut. We offer Artist credits to cover the cost of hosting the artwork in a Brand Gallery for two years.

Digital Piracy Protection:
Only the brand owners can view artwork in their galleries. Brand Galleries are designed this way to help commercial photographers who yearn for greater digital piracy and intellectual property protection. Brand Galleries are an alternative to the prevailing contributor platforms that typically give away unrestricted online access and even allow free embedding of artists’ stock photography.Privacy Built In for Artists

Mane Page is a social network with privacy built in. Mane Page never asks for personally identifiable information like real names and email addresses. So how does the brand owner contact the artist to show interest in using their uploaded artwork? Artist’s can tag (# hashtag) their uploaded artwork with information that gives the brand owner information about how to contact the artist.

Workflow:
Mane Page mobile app has an upload feature. All other aspects of the photographer’s workflow (e.g. taking photos, shoot documentation, post-processing editing, releases, licensing & copyright, etc.) are always the responsibility of the artist.

Releases:
It is always the responsibility of the artist to have available model releases, property releases, witnesses and any other necessary information about a shoot that might be required by the brand owner.
