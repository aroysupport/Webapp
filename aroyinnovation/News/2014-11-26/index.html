Aroy Innovation Opens Shop in New York

Sales office established to launch a new digital media property in early 2015

New York—November 26, 2014 — Aroy Innovation has opened their first sales office in the heart of New York City to be within arms reach for many of the world’s leading advertisers.

“We’re writing the next chapter in our exciting journey,” said Yesenia Taveras, account executive for Aroy Innovation. “We place great value on face-to-face meetings and close relationships with brand owners. Being in New York City is essential. We found a great location in Midtown Manhattan, just a few steps from the towering digital screens and flashing billboards of Times Square.”

In early 2015, Aroy Innovation will introduce it’s first digital media property to build awareness, mobile presence, loyalty, and online-to-offline conversions for brand owners. The name and more details will be released soon.

For more information and the latest news about our digital solutions, visit aroyinnovation.com

About Aroy Innovation


Aroy Innovation LLC was founded in December 2013 to launch digital media properties that connect brands and consumers in amazing ways. Our digital solutions have privacy built in, with the goal of helping brands and their clientele build a more trusted connection.
