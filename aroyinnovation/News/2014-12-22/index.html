Call to artists: Brand Galleries and Artists Gallery digital exhibitions on Mane Page social network



NEW YORK—December 22, 2014—Aroy Innovation invites artists to submit their digital artwork to safe, private online Brand Galleries through company’s Mane Page social network and keep 100% of any proceeds earned. Brand Galleries are one of the many unique features of the new social network. Brand Galleries are set to launch on January 15, 2015.

The company curates the Brand Galleries to select three outstanding images daily to be featured in the Artists Gallery on the Mane Page social network for a limited time of 60 days.

“We are throwing our skin in the game,” said Steven James Kaiser, Aroy Innovation’s company president and founder. “Our new online service is designed to help commercial photographers who yearn for alternatives to contributor business models in the era of UGC. Brand Galleries will allow a diverse, talented network of photographers to post previews of their best shots directly to brand owners and have full control over artwork pricing and licensing.”

It’s free for any artist to post any two pieces of digital artwork to safe, private online Brand Galleries where it will be hosted for two years. To ensure artists always keep 100% of any proceeds earned, the company offers Artist Credits for additional posts.

“We’ve pushed the administrative costs very low and introduce the concept of safe, private online Brand Galleries,” said Kaiser. “With Brand Galleries, aspiring artists can have their artwork posted directly to brand owners for less then 5 cents an image. The commercial photographer can take advantage of a great bulk offer that drops the price under 1 cent per post.”

All images selected for the Artists Gallery earn free Artist Credits.
