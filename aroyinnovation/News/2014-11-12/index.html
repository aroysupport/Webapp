Aroy Innovation Starts Up in Hong Kong

Technology development hub established in Hong Kong Science Park

HONG KONG—November 12, 2014— Aroy Innovation Company Limited recently moved into new office space at Hong Kong Science Park, continuing to tap into Hong Kong’s strong technology and startup scene.

“The technology community in Hong Kong is really vibrant,” said Calvin Ho, a young developer working for Aroy Innovation in Hong Kong. “After studying in the U.S. for the past few years, it’s impressive to see all the startup energy and ambitious new technology ideas percolating in Hong Kong. There’s also a great pipeline of tech and startup events in Hong Kong hosted by the big incumbent technology players and of course at the startup hubs; Paperclip HK is one excellent example of a startup campus where great events are being held regularly that we love to attend.”

Aroy Innovation recently joined the 18 months Incu-App Programme operated by Hong Kong Science and Technology Parks Corporation (HKSTPC). Our company will receive support from the programme including office space and facilities, technical and management assistance, promotion and development, business support and financial aid package. 

In early 2015, Aroy Innovation will introduce it’s first digital media property to build awareness, mobile presence, loyalty, and online-to-offline conversions for brand owners. The name and more details will be released soon.

For more information and the latest news about our digital solutions, visit aroyinnovation.com
