Mane Page social networking digital media property to launch in early 2015

Mane Page is a fast, new, safe destination where audiences discover and connect to leading brands with privacy built in

NEW YORK—December 10, 2014— Mane Page, a new social networking digital media property from Aroy Innovation LLC, is set to launch in the U.S. in early 2015.

“Mane Page is our first lightning bolt,” said Steven James Kaiser, company president and founder of Aroy Innovation. “Mane Page gives advertisers a fresh new way to grow and connect to their fan base. It’s social networking, branded.”

Mane Page is a fast, new, safe destination where audiences discover and connect to leading brands with privacy built in. Mane Page helps people explore and connect to brands without any fear of being tracked, spammed, or hacked. Mane Page never asks for personally identifiable information like real names and email addresses. Mane Page is safe, open, and opt in—consumers don’t need to be invited, sign in, sign up, or register at all.

“We believe ‘privacy built in’ features are increasingly important to consumers,” said Kaiser. “With privacy as our starting point, Mane Page is transparent and forward-looking, making no attempt to unravel a consumer’s digital past. It’s a simple approach we call True Choice, True Energy. When a consumer connects to a brand the experience should be authentic, safe, relevant, and engaging.”
