Steve Betat to Join Aroy Innovation as Global Director of Technology Delivery



HONG KONG—December 19, 2014—Aroy Innovation today announced that Steve Betat will join the company as global director of Technology Delivery, reporting to Aroy Innovation company president and founder Steven James Kaiser. Betat comes to Aroy Innovation from The McGraw-Hill Companies, where he was director at J.D. Power, a global marketing information services company. He will be responsible for Aroy Innovation’s technology strategy and the continued development of digital media solutions for markets around the world.

“Technology delivery is the most important thing we do,” said Steven James Kaiser, Aroy Innovation’s company president and founder. “Steve brings a laser focus to delivering advanced digital media technology to our audiences and clients. We are certain our team’s commitment to offer incredible products with privacy built in and rich brand engagement will thrive under Steve’s direction.”

Prior to joining Aroy Innovation, Betat spent over 15 years at J.D. Power where he lead teams that implemented and executed large, client-facing information systems projects both in the U.S. and in Singapore. He holds a degree in Management Information Systems (MIS) from California State University.
